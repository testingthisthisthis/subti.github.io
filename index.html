About Subti

Subti is an innovative AI-powered language assistance tool designed to help you communicate more effectively. Whether you're writing emails, documents, or messages, Subti provides intelligent suggestions and corrections without interrupting your workflow.

Our mission is to make language assistance accessible, seamless, and privacy-focused. With support for both MacOS and Windows, Subti integrates naturally into your daily computing experience.
